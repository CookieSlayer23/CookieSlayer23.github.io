Programming Projects

I've been apart of multiple different programming projects throughout the year. One of them being Easter Sunday. The idea with that program is pretty much trying to figure out what day Easter Sunday will fall on in a specific year and every day is different depending on the year. Basically the user just enters a year, and the program will generate the date that Easter Sunday will fall on that specific year. Another project that I was fortunate to be apart of was the Game of Pig. In the Game of Pig, there are 2 players and they take turns rolling a 6 sided dice and whoever hits 100 points first wins the game.
